Valorant Agent Comps Comparison

A dynamic Google Sheets tool designed to streamline esports analysis for live Valorant commentary. This project automatically tracks the last agents selected by all professional teams across franchised league maps. Using a Python scraper, raw data is collected and exported to CSV, then processed via Google Apps Script to populate the sheets.

For each map, a focus area allows users to select five agents, highlighting matches in green and mismatches in red for instant visual analysis. A matching score provides a quick overview of agent similarity across teams. This setup enables quick identification to assist to takling points of team strategies, prediction of plays based on agent picks and data-backed insights for live commentary.

Commentary History Tracker

A custom Google Sheets system designed to capture and analyze every esports match I commentate per year. I log details such as game, date, tournament title (with a direct link), teams, map scores, casting partners, and notes. Automated formulas and conditional formatting instantly highlight winners in green and losers in red, making match insights quick and intuitive.

The tracker also includes a dedicated stats dashboard that breaks down rounds casted, observed, and analyzed across Valorant and Counter-Strike.
